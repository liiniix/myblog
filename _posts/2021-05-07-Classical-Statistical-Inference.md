## Maximum Liklihood Estimation
- Model with unknown parameters: $X \sim p_X(x;\theta)$
- Pick $\theta$ that makes data most likely: $$ \theta_{ML} = \arg\max_{\theta} p_X(x;\theta)$$

## Desiarable Properties
- Unbiased: $E[\Theta] = \theta$
